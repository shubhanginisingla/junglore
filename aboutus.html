Wildlife Innovation

Our Journey

Three wildlife enthusiasts created Junglore to eliminate the frustration of missed wildlife sightings and poorly planned safaris. Leveraging innovation, technology, and expertise, they address the pain points of the jungle explorers. Using AI, predictive models, educational resources, and essential products, they strive to make wildlife sightings a wonderful, memorable experience.

Our Vision

We aspire to make jungle expeditions a deeply enriching experience for wildlife enthusiasts worldwide. Through innovative practices, AI, and predictive models, we want to expand the horizons of jungle exploration beyond wildlife sightings, developing deeper connections with Nature for the greater benefit of humanity and biodiversity.
